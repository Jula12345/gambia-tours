🦅 Tours & Experiences

What's the best time for bird watching?

Peak season is November-April when migratory species arrive. Our Abuko tours spot 40-60 species in 8 hours, with equipment provided.

Can I customize my tour?

Absolutely! We create custom itineraries based on your interests - wildlife, culture, photography, or adventure. Just specify in the reservation notes.

Are tours suitable for all ages?

Most tours accommodate all fitness levels. Bird watching involves gentle walking, city tours include some walking, and we can adapt for mobility needs.

✈️ Travel Information

Do I need a visa for Gambia?

Most visitors receive visa-on-arrival. Check current requirements for your nationality. We can provide invitation letters if needed.

What should I bring on tours?

Comfortable shoes, sun hat, water bottle, and camera. For bird watching: binoculars provided, but bring telephoto lens if you have one.

Is it safe to travel in Gambia?

Gambia is one of West Africa's safest countries. Our experienced local guides ensure your safety and provide cultural insights throughout your journey.
