Featured Bird Species You'll See

Green Turaco

Spectacular emerald-colored bird with distinctive red flight feathers. Endemic to West Africa.

Malachite Kingfisher

Brilliant blue and orange kingfisher, often seen diving for fish in the reserve's waters.

Senegal Parrot

Intelligent green parrot with yellow head and orange belly, national bird of Senegal.

Yellow-crowned Gonolek

Striking black and red bird with yellow crown, known for its melodious call.

African Grey Hornbill

Large bird with distinctive curved bill, important seed disperser in the forest ecosystem.

Purple Heron

Elegant wading bird with purple-grey plumage, master of patient fishing technique.
